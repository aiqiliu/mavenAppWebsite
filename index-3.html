Northwestern Maven

The Northwestern MAVEN app is an internal app for rating marketing vendors used by different marketing departments at Northwestern University.

Open link from your phone

It requires a Northwestern login, and is designed for marketing and PR departments of NU as a way to assemble vendor impressions and reviews, and elevate the quality of NU branding.

The app seeks to unites the multiple vendor ratings list that exist within each department into one general databases of reviews and recommendations.

The app offers a way to search for vendors, by keywords or categories. To review vendors from the database, or even to add new vendors. We offer a way to bookmark favorite vendors and to discover new vendors through our "recently reviewed" section.

Installing on an iOS device

This app is an Enterprise app, which requires an extra step to be installed. Once you have downloaded the app, you will need to authorize Northwestern as a trusted developer.

Step 1: Install App from website

Step 2: You may receive a warning when trying to access the app

Step 3: Go to "Setting -> General -> Profile"

Step 4: Choose "Northwestern University"

Step 5: Choose "Trust"
